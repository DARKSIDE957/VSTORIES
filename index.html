About Me

I'm Virus - a name that reflects my fascination with the hidden and the mysterious, both in technology and storytelling.

I hold a high diploma in Cyber Security, which has given me a unique lens through which to view the world. While I’ve chosen not to pursue that field professionally, the discipline and mindset it required continue to shape the way I approach creativity and problem-solving.

My true calling, however, is writing. I craft stories that dive deep into emotion, morality, and the choices that define us. My debut story, The Road to Hell, marks the beginning of my journey as an author - a tale born from imagination, personal reflection, and a love for powerful narratives.

More stories are already on the way, each one exploring new worlds, characters, and ideas. If you're looking for thoughtful storytelling and raw human experiences brought to life through words, you're in the right place.
